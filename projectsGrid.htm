Welcome to my project portfolio — a collection of interactive technologies and learning experiences designed to expand human connection, knowledge, creativity, empathy and wonder. From augmented reality tools for physics education, to collaborative virtual makerspaces, empathy tools and math games for kids, these projects are designed to push the boundaries of technology while addressing real-world educational challenges. These projects are usually accompanied by my scientific research studies, academic publications and presentations, which you will find in the project details.
