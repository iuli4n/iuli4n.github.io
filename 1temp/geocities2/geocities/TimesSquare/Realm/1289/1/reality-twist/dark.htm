ENLIGHTED BY DARKNESS

The everlasting darkness which I surround
Or does it envelop me
Speaks softly, again, the whispers of a wicked mind…
Creations spawned from the depths of consciousness…
The depths of the Abyss which is my being…
Why do I do this?
Why now ? why ever, does this happen to me
No I do not ask for them to come… the urge to express these feelings,
Produced by the ebony darkness…
The strands of wisdom which flow from the night
They which give me these wonderful illusions from which
I write.
I do not wish for it to stop.
I require just a simple answer…
Why?
Why not some other time… why this place, this time?
The feelings that wrap around me, where do they come from?
They urge me to express myself, now more than ever, creating
The fanatic addiction… addiction to illusion,
The Illusion of the Mind
Ink is light compared to this dense darkness
The same darkness that flares these feelings…
Into me, my head…. But WHY?!
Why do I do this ?
Why now ?
How have I been blessed in such a way that I may receive these
Wonderful gifts of creation?
It is time to stop, I know it, for I cannot…
It urges me to go on, create and express, explode, shatter my imagination
Into this bit of paper… The paper… which does not even exist in physical form
The imagination… It is time to stop NOW more than ever, for I am talking
Nonsense
Or is it?
Probably… probably art ? probably not ? Stop !
The fiends will return,
Another day.
