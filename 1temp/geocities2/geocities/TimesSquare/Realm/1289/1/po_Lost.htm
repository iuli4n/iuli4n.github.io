Lost

 

In the hug you follow, a beautiful dance...

Her face close to you, her eyes killing you, her soft lips breathing softly by your ear.

Both tired... Better to lay down before something is broken.

 

You sit first… Two are on the bed: one sleeping, one caring of the other, in her sleep.

The edge is fine; you sit on the outside leaning it. That of the taken, soft bed.

 

Relax, slide down.

Only my head resting on the edge, turning to see she is already on the night floor.

Both tired, both not knowing…

 

She slides up to you, her beautiful face inviting you… her hair whispering, taunting you.

Your hands go to her face, around her smooth cheeks, her moist mouth opens, her eyes close softly; the hands past her silk cheeks, around her ears.

The soft fine hair drips between your fingers. Powder, soft vanilla could not be sweeter.

 

Up, down, flowing… all around.

Your fingers sliding, your hands caressing the tired angel; softly they flow through the velvet field, time to time they dip back into the quiet lake of her cheeks.

Too soft… so beautiful. Her eyes bloom open, soft flowers upon the landscape.

Silent, slow not to break the spell, she lowers. Looking for rest, her head floats down to rest on your chest.

Past her soft cheek, through her vanilla hair, your hand flows still.

A hand around your chest, crawls, a caterpillar, sliding softly up… Her eyes close once again, her lips break apart into a sigh of thousand words.

 

Slow breaths…

Silent. Beautiful night envelops.

Your hand breaks off the journey from the strands, slides around her waist. A kiss lands like a gentile snowflake upon the cheek.

Your heart beats once. Love floods.

 

And as you stare down, the darkness parts. You see light. Beauty.

This feeling, never preceded. Always in the darkness, never has anyone cared.

And now you sit and think through the shadow light. An angel on your chest. Sleeping silent.

“Tick”

The time flows, a constant reminder of the time lost. Ever so much; always had the chance but never followed it. Fate?

 

Beautiful shape...

And just when you think nothing can conquer this feeling,

Just when you thought the beauty is asleep,

Her silk body moves.

A dream intruded on by thoughts? I apologize.

The hand around your chest grips more. She hugs more tightly.

The head turns softly up, up towards painful eyes.

She find them, and yours sink into hers… a second… no words needed…  two seconds… for an eternity in those eyes I would give my soul. Softly they close, locking it in. I am lost.

She lays back, the hug continues, never broken.

 

She floats away,

Both breath by breath, sigh by sigh, strand by strand, softness by silence….

Sink on the shores of Love.
